Birjand City

Birjand is the capital of the Iranian province of South Khorasan.
The city is known for its saffron, barberry, jujube, and handmade carpet exports.
Birjand had a population of 250,000 in 2020.

This city has a very old history and has been the center of science and culture for many years .
Many celebrities and astronomers in different sciences are born in this city
